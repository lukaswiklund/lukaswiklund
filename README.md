### 🚀 Some of the things I'm building

**[Forento](https://forento.io)** — an all-in-one platform for coaches, creators, and entrepreneurs to sell online courses, memberships, digital downloads, and coaching programs.

**[BRF Radar](https://brfradar.se/)** — a financial analysis tool for Swedish condominium associations to understand their financial health before buying a property.

**[Kärnkraft i Sverige](https://nuclear.lukaswiklund.se/en)** — a real-time monitoring dashboard for Swedish nuclear power plants.

**[Sveriges Ekonomi](https://sverigesekonomi.se/)** — a comprehensive overview of local tax rates in different municipalities in Sweden.
